{%- comment -%} Assigns `covered_ids` — every threat id that at least one community record claims to mitigate, as a pipe-delimited string. Test coverage with: {%- assign needle = "|" | append: t.id | append: "|" -%} {%- if covered_ids contains needle -%} ... covered ... {%- endif -%} The pipe delimiters are load-bearing. Liquid's `contains` is a substring test on strings, and threat ids are prefixes of one another — "slsa-v1-2-b1" is a substring of "slsa-v1-2-b12". Without the delimiters, B1 would report as covered whenever B12 was. Requires `records`, so include community-records.html first. {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign covered_ids = "|" -%} {%- for r in records -%} {%- for c in r.threats -%} {%- assign covered_ids = covered_ids | append: c.id | append: "|" -%} {%- endfor -%} {%- endfor -%}
